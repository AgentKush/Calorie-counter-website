# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

Mycelia is a small, friendly open-source project. Everyone who takes part —
reporting bugs, suggesting ideas, or contributing code — is welcome, and we
want the experience to be a positive one for all involved.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful, patient and considerate in issues, pull requests and reviews.
- Assume good intent and give constructive, kind feedback.
- Stay on topic and keep discussion focused on improving the project.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Personal attacks, insults, or demeaning comments.
- Harassment of any kind, public or private.
- Sharing others' private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make someone feel unwelcome or unsafe.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull requests and
any related communication — and when representing the project elsewhere.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, please report it to the
maintainer at **finlayhansford66@gmail.com**. Reports will be reviewed and
handled in confidence, as fairly and promptly as possible.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit or remove comments, commits, issues and other
contributions that don't follow this code, and may warn or block anyone whose
behaviour is judged inappropriate, harmful, or repeatedly disruptive.
